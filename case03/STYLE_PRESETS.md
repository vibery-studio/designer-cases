# STYLE PRESETS
Reference file cho Portfolio Mentor Prompt

---

## Cách dùng

File này chứa các aesthetic directions. AI đọc để:
1. Hiểu vibe và cảm hứng của từng style
2. Tự chọn fonts, colors, effects phù hợp
3. Match style với profile của user

**Không cần follow chính xác** - đây là điểm khởi đầu để sáng tạo.

## MINIMALIST SPECTRUM

### A. Swiss Precision
Surgical clean. Grid là tôn giáo. Typography làm tất cả heavy lifting. Helvetica hoặc tương đương. Đen trắng + một màu accent đỏ hoặc xanh dương. Không có gì thừa. Mỗi pixel có lý do tồn tại.

**Vibe:** Dieter Rams gặp Swiss poster design
**Best for:** Senior designers, enterprise targets, những người muốn nói "tôi serious về craft"

### B. Japanese Zen
Ma (negative space) là nhân vật chính. Asymmetric balance. Màu muted - off-white, charcoal, một chút sage green. Serif + sans-serif pairing. Fade-in chậm rãi. Breathing room ở khắp nơi.

**Vibe:** Muji store gặp Kinfolk magazine
**Best for:** Thoughtful designers, wellness/lifestyle brands, người coi trọng process hơn flash

### C. Nordic Calm  
Hygge digital. Warm neutrals - cream, soft gray, wood tones. Rounded corners nhẹ nhàng. Soft shadows. Feels like a cozy coffee shop. Friendly nhưng vẫn professional.

**Vibe:** Copenhagen studio vào mùa đông
**Best for:** Product designers, Scandinavian market, người muốn approachable mà không childish

---

## BOLD & EXPRESSIVE

### D. Brutalist Soft
Raw structure nhưng không aggressive. Concrete texture. Bold borders. Uppercase headings. Terracotta và earth tones thay vì pure black. Harsh shadows on hover. Grid nhưng break grid có chủ đích.

**Vibe:** Tadao Ando architecture gặp zine culture
**Best for:** Agency roles, creative studios, người muốn stand out khỏi sea of sameness

### E. Editorial Luxury
Magazine spread làm portfolio. Didone/serif typography to bự. Asymmetric layouts. Dark mode với gold accents. Plenty of whitespace (hoặc blackspace). Elegant reveals. Feels expensive.

**Vibe:** Vogue gặp Kinfolk gặp luxury brand campaign
**Best for:** Fashion tech, luxury brands, senior roles muốn thể hiện sophistication

### F. Neo Graffiti
Street art meets digital. Electric colors - lime, hot pink, cyan. Bold display fonts. Glitch effects. Neon glows. Không sợ loud. Gradient text. Spray paint textures nếu muốn.

**Vibe:** Tokyo street fashion gặp cyberpunk
**Best for:** Creative agencies, gaming, youth brands, người có personality mạnh

## RETRO & NOSTALGIC

### G. Retro Futurism '99
Y2K nostalgia. CRT scanlines. Monospace fonts. Cyan + magenta. Chrome gradients. Typing animations. Pixel transitions. Early internet aesthetic nhưng modern execution.

**Vibe:** The Matrix gặp old Apple website gặp vaporwave
**Best for:** Tech roles, gaming industry, millennial audience thích nostalgia

### H. Vintage Film
35mm warmth. Film grain overlay. Sepia và amber tones. Aged paper backgrounds. Serif typography. Polaroid-style frames cho images. Subtle vignette. Analog authenticity.

**Vibe:** Wes Anderson color palette gặp darkroom
**Best for:** Photography-heavy portfolios, storytelling focus, authentic personal brand
**2025 Trend:** "Sunkissed Sierra" - warm earthy orange đang hot. Nostalgic, comforting, như ánh nắng chiều. Brands dùng để feel "crafted with care, approachable yet quality".

### I. Art Deco Revival
Gatsby glamour. Geometric patterns - sunbursts, chevrons, fan shapes. Gold + navy + emerald. Decorative borders. Poiret-style typography. Feels like a luxury hotel lobby.

**Vibe:** 1920s New York gặp modern luxury
**Best for:** Hospitality design, distinctive personality, người muốn memorable first impression

---

## ORGANIC & NATURAL

### J. Organic Flow
Blob shapes. Curved everything. Earth tones - sage, terracotta, sand, olive. Morphing animations. Natural textures. Rounded corners everywhere. Feels human, not machine-made.

**Vibe:** Wellness brand gặp sustainability report
**Best for:** Human-centered design, wellness industry, người focus vào empathy
**2025 Trend:** "Flower Power" - abstract botanicals, geometric florals thay vì realistic flowers. Clean, modern interpretation of nature. Kết hợp với warm palette (terracotta, amber) để tạo "instant warmth and positivity".

### K. Paper Craft
Handmade feel. Paper textures. Kraft brown và cream. Handwritten font accents. Stamp/sticker decorations. Tape elements. Scrapbook energy nhưng organized.

**Vibe:** Bullet journal gặp indie zine
**Best for:** Fresh graduates, education sector, approachable creative roles
**2025 Trend:** "Type by Hand" - handwritten lettering comeback mạnh. Organic typography mang human touch vào digital. Imperfect letterforms = authenticity, playfulness, emotional warmth. Đặc biệt hot trong food, beverage, craft industries.

## TECH & FUTURISTIC

### L. Cyber Gradient
Mesh gradients - purple to blue to teal. Glass morphism cards. Dark mode. Subtle glow effects. 3D transforms on hover. Feels like a fintech app.

**Vibe:** Linear app gặp Stripe dashboard
**Best for:** Fintech, AI/ML, modern startups, người muốn "contemporary tech" vibe

### M. Terminal Hacker
CLI aesthetic. Green on black (hoặc amber on black). Monospace everything. Typing animations. Command prompt decorations. Code blocks. Matrix references optional.

**Vibe:** Developer's natural habitat
**Best for:** Design engineers, technical roles, developer-heavy teams

---

## PLAYFUL & CREATIVE

### N. Memphis Pop
80s revival. Bold geometric shapes. Primary colors + pastels. Patterns everywhere. Bouncy animations. Confetti. Wiggle effects. Unserious in the best way.

**Vibe:** Memphis Milano gặp Nickelodeon
**Best for:** Kids brands, playful startups, fresh graduates với energetic personality

### O. Clay 3D
Soft 3D aesthetic. Pastel colors - lavender, mint, peach. Puffy shadows. Rounded everything. Float animations. Feels like touching soft clay.

**Vibe:** Modern SaaS illustration style
**Best for:** Consumer apps, friendly brands, modern approachable look

## ADVANCED EFFECTS (khi được yêu cầu)

Chỉ suggest khi user explicitly muốn hoặc apply cho creative developer roles:

**2025 Trend: "Designed to Move"**
Motion không còn là bonus - đang trở thành expectation. Từ subtle logo animations đến looping product demos, UI walk-throughs, kinetic typography. Motion = richer storytelling, shows craft, helps stand out. Ngay cả small moments như short loop mockups cũng elevate project significantly.

**Three.js options:**
- Particle field background - floating dots, mouse interactive
- Wave mesh - animated terrain/fabric
- 3D text - extruded typography hero

**Parallax options:**
- Depth layers - elements move at different scroll speeds  
- Perspective tilt - cards tilt on mouse move
- Scroll zoom - elements scale while scrolling

**P5.js options:**
- Noise field - flowing generative background
- Particle system - generative art vibes
- Interactive shapes - respond to cursor

**GSAP options:**
- Scroll-triggered reveals
- Text split animations (character by character)
- Smooth section transitions

## IMAGE DIRECTION

Thay vì hardcode URLs, guide AI chọn image phù hợp:

**Hero backgrounds:**
- Minimal styles → clean workspace, simple objects
- Bold styles → architecture, urban, abstract
- Organic styles → nature, textures, soft shapes
- Tech styles → gradients, abstract 3D, dark moody

**Profile photos:**
- Luôn dùng placeholder với comment rõ ràng
- Suggest style: professional headshot, environmental portrait, candid working

**Project thumbnails:**
- Nếu user có Dribbble/Behance → link ra
- Nếu không → UI mockup placeholders, device frames

**Texture overlays:**
- Paper styles → paper grain, kraft texture
- Vintage styles → film grain, dust particles
- Brutalist styles → concrete, noise

## NGUYÊN TẮC CHUNG

1. **Không generic** - Tránh Inter, Roboto, purple gradient on white
2. **Commit to direction** - Chọn một aesthetic rồi đi đến cùng
3. **Match complexity với vision** - Minimalist = restraint, Maximalist = elaborate
4. **Typography first** - Font choice định hình 50% cảm giác
5. **Details matter** - Hover states, transitions, micro-interactions
